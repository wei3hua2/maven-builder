# 🏡 Maven Home — AI-Powered Homeowner Companion

## Building Transparency. Delivering Peace of Mind.

Maven Home is an AI-powered mobile experience designed to give homeowners full visibility, clarity, and control throughout their construction journey — from planning to post-handover.

---

## 🚩 The Problem

Homeowners often face:
- Lack of visibility into project progress  
- Confusing technical jargon  
- Unclear cost breakdowns  
- Slow communication with contractors  
- No structured support after project completion  

---

## 💡 The Solution

**Maven Home** combines mobile technology with AI to create a seamless, transparent, and intelligent homeowner experience.

---

### 🔄 Real-Time Project Timeline
Track construction progress with clear, visual updates:
- Weekly milestones  
- Photo updates  
- AI-generated summaries
